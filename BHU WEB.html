Banaras Hindu University

Banaras Hindu University is an internationally reputed temple of learning, situated in the holy city of Varanasi. This creative and innovative university was founded by the great nationalist leader, Pandit Madan Mohan Malaviya, in 1916, through close cooperation with great personalities like Dr Annie Besant, who viewed it as the University of India. Banaras Hindu University was established by the Parliamentary legislation-B.H.U. Act 1915.

National Seminar on Innovative Strategies and Sustainable Efforts for Viksit Bharat through Social Inclusion

Social inclusion is a fundamental pillar of Viksit Bharat approach.According to World Bank “Social inclusion is the process of improving the terms on which individuals and groups take part in society—improving the ability, opportunity, and dignity of those disadvantaged on the basis of their identity”. In this context, the Governmentalso aims at to create a society where every person, regardless of their socio-economic background, gender, ethnicity, or physical disability gets an equal opportunity to participate in and get benefits from national progress.The ‘Viksit Bharat@2047’is the agenda of Government of India to establish India as a fully developed nation by its 100thanniversary of its independence in 2047. This comprehensive plan extends to important areas like social equality, economic development, environment, digital empowerment, and effective governance etc., are integral parts which are helping in building a prosperous, inclusive, and resilient India.

FEB 03

International Conference on Environment and Sustainable Development ICESD – 2025

The International Conference on Environment and Sustainable Development, convened by the Institute of Environment and Sustainable Development (IESD) at Banaras Hindu University, serves as a platform for addressing pressing global environmental challenges and promoting sustainable development strategies. The conference encompasses a broad spectrum of critical themes, including environmental issues, sustainable technologies, food security, climate change, biotechnology, and governance. It endeavours to explore solutions for managing pollution, urbanisation, biodiversity loss, and other environmental crises, while also advancing discussions on nature-based solutions, renewable energy, and sustainable agricultural practices. Furthermore, the conference emphasises the significance of national and international policies, legal frameworks, and ethical considerations in achieving environmental sustainability. By facilitating interdisciplinary dialogue among experts and stakeholders, the conference fosters collaboration and innovative approaches to advancing the objectives of sustainable development.
